Community Impact

Foundation Activities

Engaging our community through diverse spiritual, social, and cultural events across the globe.

Institutional Excellence

Hanuma-Madhwa Award

A prestigious annual recognition honouring individuals who embody the values and teachings of Hanuma and Madhwacharya — celebrating outstanding contribution to spiritual leadership and community service.

Yearly Archive

Cultural Recognition

Madhwa Vijaya Award

Celebrating excellence in the propagation of Madhwa philosophy and Vaishnava traditions. This award recognises scholars, writers, and community leaders who have advanced the teachings of Madhwacharya.

Yearly Archive

Honour & Award

Madhweshanugraha Honor and Award

Bestowed in the grace of Madhwesha (Lord Vishnu), this honour recognises devout individuals and institutions that have rendered distinguished service to the Madhwa community and its spiritual heritage.

Spiritual Journeys

Pilgrimage Tours

Organising guided sacred journeys to revered temples and holy sites such as Udupi Krishna Matha, Mantralayam, and Gokarna — nurturing spiritual growth and community bonding.

Honour & Award

Bheema Madhwesha Honor and Award

In honour of Bheemasena — a revered figure in Madhwa tradition — this award is conferred upon those who exemplify strength, righteousness, and unwavering commitment to the values of the Madhwa faith.

Community Welfare

Medical Camps

Providing free healthcare access to the community through regular medical camps, blood donation drives, specialist consultations, and wellness workshops — service as a form of devotion.
