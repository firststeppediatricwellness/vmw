VMWF is a Registered Charitable National Organisation dedicated to the welfare of the Madhwa community. Rooted in the timeless teachings of Sri Madhwacharya, we connect thousands of families across India through spirituality, social service, and modern welfare programs.

Our Vision

A global, self-sustaining community network that preserves our spiritual values while excelling in modern social and economic spheres.

Our Mission

Empowering community members through education, job placement, matrimonial assistance, spiritual development, and cultural celebrations.
